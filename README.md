# UniDive 2nd Training School in Yerevan, Armenia

This is the repository for the [UniDive 2nd Training School](https://unidive.lisn.upsaclay.fr/doku.php?id=meetings:other-events:2nd_unidive_training_school) in Yerevan, Armenia, held on 20-24 January 2026. 

## Trainee groups
For parts of course 2 (LLMS for Low-Resourced Languages) and 3 (Diversity quantification in NLP), trainees will have group assignments for partactical work. 
Groups were designed so as to ensure diversity of members in terms of country of affiliation, gender, and career stage. See the [list of groups](Yerevan-winter-school-trainee-groups.pdf).
